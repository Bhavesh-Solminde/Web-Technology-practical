India Gate is a war memorial designed by Edwin Lutyens. It honors Indian soldiers who died in World War I. Names of over 13,000 soldiers are inscribed on it. The Amar Jawan Jyoti flame burns beneath it. It is a major tourist attraction in Delhi.
